How We Met

"We met? Well... we were born into the same family!" Yep, plot twist of the century.

Never in our wildest dreams did we imagine we’d go from awkward “salam-dua” to shaadi ke phoolon ke beech vows.

We were more like background extras in each other’s lives, just a polite nod at weddings, a shared eye-roll over aunties' gossip, and the occasional battle for the last gulab jamun.

But then came Talha’s wedding. (Kon Talha? Our cousin, who had absolutely no clue the next wedding he’d be attending would be ours.)

Somewhere between loud dhol beats, mehndi madness, and probably a very overcooked kebab, something changed. A glance turned into a spark, a spark into a silent promise...

and by the time the rasmalai was served, we both knew... "Yeh toh ho gaya hai." ❤️
